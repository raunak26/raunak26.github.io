About Me

Hi, my name is Raunak Anand. I’m currently a Junior at the University of California, Davis, planning to pursue a bachelors degree in Computer Science and Statistics - Machine Learning track. I am a passionate coder, with the constant willingness to learn new things. I have had three internships and have taken up an on-campus job as IT Service Desk Analyst to support myself on campus. Besides this, I am very passionate about soccer; in fact, I have captained my team to national-level competitions several times, while also being scouted by a Bundesliga club called Hoffenhiem FC. This sport has made me the team player I am today.

I like staying involved in campus activities such as Campus clubs or socials. I am part of various clubs on campus - the Davis Computer Science Club, Blockchain@Davis, UC Davis Artificial Intelligence Society, and the Finance and Investment Club at UC Davis. The reason for my involvement in so many clubs is because of the immense variety of people you meet and knowledge you gain from them. Joining the Finance and Investment Club at UC Davis has probably been most insightful for me, given the type of people that get selected into the club, the guidance you receive from the club members, and the mentorship you receive from your seniors. Their influence has allowed me to take up another project of building a Robinhood bot using its API to invest into stocks based on data receieved from the same. I also have a keen interest in Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. Personally, I feel that you must acquire the knowledge on the background and manufacturing of technology before moving on to its implementations in society. I am eager to find solutions to various problems through the same mindset and, also, see it as a huge business opportunity. Given this approach, I am always trying to network with people in order to get new ideas.
